About Noxolo Duma

A Clinician, Researcher & Advocate for Improved Rehabilitation Services

Noxolo Erica Duma is the Founder and Director of Noxolo Duma Physiotherapy. She holds a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from the University of KwaZulu-Natal and a Master's in Public Health, completed in 2019 under the supervision of the late Professor TE Madiba. She is currently completing her Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) under the supervision of Professor KW Hlongwana and Dr NA Benjamin-Damons, reflecting her strong commitment to academic excellence and evidence-based practice.

Who We Are

A Clinician, Researcher & Advocate for Improved Rehabilitation Services

Noxolo Duma Physiotherapy is founded and led by Noxolo E. Duma — a research-based physiotherapist, Master of Public Health graduate, and current PhD Candidate at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN).

Noxolo's doctoral research focuses on developing a Contextualised Evidence-Based Interprofessional Rehabilitation Model Responsive to the Needs of Children with Cerebral Palsy in Durban CBD and Pinetown — research that directly informs the quality of care provided at this practice.

What We Offer

Our Physiotherapy Services

Research-informed treatment with a focus on the use of the following modalities — grounded in the latest clinical evidence.

👶

Paediatric Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy for children with neurological conditions such as cerebral palsy, developmental delays, general paediatric rehabilitation for fractures and injuries.

🧠

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our approach is grounded in the latest clinical research and interprofessional rehabilitation frameworks.

🤲

Manual Therapy

Hands-on physiotherapy techniques to reduce pain, restore mobility, and improve quality of life for patients of all ages.

🦴

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation following orthopaedic surgery and musculoskeletal injury focused on restoring strength, mobility and return-to-function through progressive loading and tailored exercise.

⚡

Electrotherapy

Targeted relief through electrotherapy techniques such as shockwave therapy, transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS), and interferential therapy, reducing pain and accelerating healing.

🫁

Respiratory Physiotherapy

Assessment and treatment to improve ventilation, airway clearance and functional respiratory capacity, helpful for chronic and acute respiratory conditions.

⚕️

Dry Needling

This technique involves the insertion of needles into myofascial trigger points. It reduces pain, increases blood flow, and restores optimal nerve function. It relaxes tight muscles.

🔄

Joint Mobilisation & Soft Tissue

Hands-on joint mobilisation and soft tissue techniques to improve range of motion, reduce stiffness and restore comfortable movement patterns.
