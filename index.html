About Noxolo Duma

A Clinician, Researcher & Advocate for Improved Rehabilitation Services

Holding a Master of Public Health, Noxolo bridges the gap between clinical physiotherapy practice and public health, bringing a uniquely broad perspective to patient care and community-based rehabilitation.

Who We Are

A Clinician, Researcher & Advocate for Improved Rehabilitation Services

Noxolo Duma Physiotherapy is founded and led by Noxolo E. Duma — a research-based physiotherapist, Master of Public Health graduate, and current PhD Candidate at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN).

Noxolo's doctoral research focuses on developing a Contextualised Evidence-Based Interprofessional Rehabilitation Model Responsive to the Needs of Children with Cerebral Palsy in KwaZulu-Natal — research that directly informs the quality of care provided at this practice.

With expertise spanning paediatric rehabilitation, neurological conditions, public health, mental health, and community-based rehabilitation, this practice offers a uniquely comprehensive and evidence-informed approach to physiotherapy care.

What We Offer

Our Physiotherapy Services

Research-informed treatment with a focus on the use of the following modalities — grounded in the latest clinical evidence.

👶

Paediatric Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy for children, including those with Cerebral Palsy, developmental delays, and paediatric neurological conditions. Physiotherapy for children with neurological conditions such as cerebral palsy, developmental delays, general paediatric rehabilitation for fractures and injuries.

🧠

Neurological Rehabilitation

Assessment and treatment of neurological conditions affecting movement, balance, and functional independence. Evidence-based approaches for stroke, brain injury, Parkinson's, and more. Our approach is grounded in the latest clinical research and interprofessional rehabilitation frameworks.

🤲

Manual Therapy

Hands-on physiotherapy techniques — mobilisation, manipulation, soft tissue release — to reduce pain and restore optimal movement and function.

🦴

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Specialist rehabilitation following total knee and hip replacements, soft tissue injuries, and musculoskeletal conditions — using evidence-based techniques to restore strength, mobility, and function.

⚡

Electrotherapy

Targeted relief through electrotherapy techniques such as shockwave therapy, transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS), and interferential therapy — reducing pain and accelerating healing.

🫁

Respiratory Physiotherapy

Respiratory physio helps manage conditions like asthma, pneumonia, COPD, and post-operative respiratory complications — helping you work towards optimal lung function.

💉

Dry Needling

This technique involves the insertion of needles into myofascial trigger points. It reduces pain, increases blood flow, and restores optimal nerve function — also known as Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS).

🔄

Joint Mobilisation & Soft Tissue

Skilled manual techniques targeting joint stiffness and soft tissue restrictions — improving range of motion, reducing pain, and enhancing overall movement quality.
